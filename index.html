Overview

Abstract

FinDebate is a retrieval-augmented multi-agent framework designed for institutional-style financial analysis from earnings call transcripts. The system combines domain-specific retrieval, specialized financial analyst agents, and a collaborative debate mechanism to improve report quality, factual grounding, and investment consistency.

Evaluated on 64 Earnings2Insights transcripts across five large language models, FinDebate achieves consistent improvements averaging approximately +0.14 score points over zero-shot baselines on a four-point evaluation scale.
